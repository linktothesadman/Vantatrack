Upload Campaign Data
Upload CSV files containing campaign data from Facebook, Google, ShareIT, or other platforms.
Required CSV Format

Your CSV file must include these columns:

  • client_email - Email address of the client
  • campaign_name - Name of the campaign
  • platform - Platform name (Facebook, Google, ShareIT, etc.)
  • date - Date in YYYY-MM-DD format
  • impressions - Number of impressions
  • clicks - Number of clicks
  • spent - Amount spent
  • reach - Number of people reached
  • budget - Campaign budget (optional)
  • status - Campaign status (optional)
Need Help?

Having trouble with your CSV format? Here are some tips:

  • Ensure all required columns are present
  • Use YYYY-MM-DD date format
  • Numbers should not contain commas
  • Email addresses must be valid
